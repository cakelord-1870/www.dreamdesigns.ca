Limited Edition Prints Organic Cotton Face Mask: Garden Cats

Limited prints organic Cotton Face Mask made in Vancouver, Canada from two layers of tightly woven 100% GOTS/Oeko-Tex certified organic cotton sateen. Check out our other organic cotton face masks.

We will donate $5 to Vancouver's Downtown Eastside Women’s Centre for each "Garden Cats" mask sold.

Each mask is individually cut by hand and has a different pattern placement. We will place at least one whole cat on each mask in a central location. You can choose from Standing Cat, Sitting Cat, and Lying Cat.  

Top opening filter pocket. Optional organic cotton sateen filter special sized for the limited edition masks. Convenient elastic ear loops with pain-free headband, adjustable to fit different face shapes and sizes. The headband also holds the mask around your neck when you don't need to wear it. Sew-in nose seal. Breathable. Washable. Reusable. Unisex.

To adjust the size of your mask, pull the knots on the elastic earloops out of the side channels, untie and then re-tie them so the mask fits snugly on the face, but not too tight that it pulls on the ears. Wash by hand with hot water and regular soap to sterilize and hang to dry. Do not microwave. 

This mask has not been tested against pathogens, including COVID-19. Please use at your own risk. To stop the spread of COVID-19, please stay home and practise social distancing first and foremost. This mask is not sufficient for people with a weakened immune system.
